# awesome-ballroom-dance

A curated list of awesome resources for **Ballroom Dance**, focused on the **10 Dances** of the International Style (5 Standard + 5 Latin): Waltz, Tango, Viennese Waltz, Slow Foxtrot, Quickstep, Cha Cha, Samba, Rumba, Paso Doble, and Jive.

## The 10 Dances

### Standard (Modern)
- Waltz
- Tango
- Viennese Waltz
- Slow Foxtrot
- Quickstep

### Latin
- Cha Cha Cha
- Samba
- Rumba
- Paso Doble
- Jive

- [Ballroom dance - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ballroom_dance) - Overview of ballroom dance history and styles.
- [The 19 Different Types of Ballroom Dance - DanceVision](https://blog.dancevision.com/the-19-different-types-of-ballroom-dance) - Breakdown of Standard, Latin, and other ballroom styles.
- [Breaking Down Competitive Ballroom Dance Styles - Dance Spirit](https://dancespirit.com/breaking-down-ballroom-styles/) - Introduction to the 10 competitive dances.

## Community & Forums

- [Dance Forums](https://www.dance-forums.com/) - General dance community with a dedicated [Ballroom Dance](https://www.dance-forums.com/forums/ballroom-dance.7/) section.
- [BallroomDancers.com Message Board](https://www.ballroomdancers.com/Message_Board/?forum=1) - Long-running discussion board for ballroom dancers.
- [Passion4Dancing Forum](https://www.passion4dancing.com/members-forum/) - Community forum for dancers worldwide.
- [r/Dance (Reddit)](https://www.reddit.com/r/Dance/) - General dance subreddit covering classes, costumes, and choreography.

## Dance Steps & Tutorials

- [LearnToDance.com](https://www.learntodance.com/online-ballroom-dance-lessons/) - Free video lessons for Waltz, Cha Cha, Rumba, Foxtrot, Tango, and more.
- [Passion4Dancing - How to Ballroom Dance](https://www.passion4dancing.com/how-to-ballroom-dance/) - Beginner guides with 500+ video lessons.
- [Ballroom Feed](https://www.ballroomfeed.com/) - Step-by-step pattern and technique tutorials.
- [Image Ballroom Dance Academy - Tutorials](https://www.imageballroomdance.com/tutorials) - Tutorials covering technique for multiple dances.
- [Basic Ballroom Dance Steps (YouTube Playlist)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLgM0bfYIGz3ReaVcbVGukGUwHeySswC52) - Video playlist of basic steps for beginners.

## Competitions & Governing Bodies

- [World DanceSport Federation (WDSF)](https://www.worlddancesport.org/) - Official IOC-recognized governing body for DanceSport.
- [World Dance Council (WDC)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Dance_Council) - Independent international dancesport organization.
- [USA Dance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USA_Dance) - National governing body for DanceSport in the United States.
- [United States Dance Championships](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Dance_Championships) - One of the major DanceSport competitions in the US.
- [WikiDanceSport](https://www.wikidancesport.com/) - Wiki covering competitions, results, and dancesport history.

## Dance Music

- [Ballroom-Music.net](https://ballroom-music.net/) - Searchable library of Standard and Latin dance music, sortable by dance and tempo.
- [Music4Dance](https://www.music4dance.net/dances/ballroom-competition-categories) - Database of songs organized by ballroom competition categories.
- [music4dance blog - Playlists for Ballroom DJs](https://music4dance.blog/2025/03/10/playlists-for-ballroom-djs/) - Tips and playlists for ballroom DJs.
- ["The Sound of Ballroom" (Spotify)](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/24FlJf5U7AJ1AnlUY99TZD) - Spotify playlist of ballroom dance music.
- [UCONN Ballroom Playlists](https://www.uconnballroom.com/members/ballroom-playlists) - Practice playlists organized by dance style and tempo.
